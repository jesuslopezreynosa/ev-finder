# Batteries

## Chemistries

### LFP - Lithium Iron Phosphate

    - Slightly less energy dense (heavider for the same amount of energy compared to other batteries), but they are more stable chemistry
    - Can be charged to 100% more often, most manufacturers recommend defaulting to a 100% charge limit for this chemistry for calibrating the BMS
    - Can usually retain 70-80% of it's original capacity after 3000-5000 cycles
    - Is not capable of triggering thermal runaway (least fire risk)
